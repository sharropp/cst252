ARCADE BUSINESS CARDS

Programs: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

This is one of several mockup renders which I created as part of a fictional business card branding project. This render depicts the branding of a hypothetical retro arcade, so I used a number of low resolution typefaces, bright colors, and square visual elements to cement this early video-game aesthetic in the design. This project was one of my early attempts at creating a brand identity, as did I continue to practice managing layout and white space while ensuring that content is quickly and easily readable.
