TASTE TEA

Programs: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

This group project tasked my teammates and I with creating a cover for a hypothetical or existing music album. We decided it would be nice if our school had an album of lofi music, a genre students often listen to while studying. The album cover was to mash up photography with digital art, so one of my group members was responsible for photography, and another was responsible for greeting the vector art of Monte (the school mascot) on the beach and in a dorm room. I was responsible for creating CSUMBeats Logo, which depicts an Otter with headphones and a record, though I also polished the overall album cover design with lighting effects, image filters, and adjustments to shadows and colors.
