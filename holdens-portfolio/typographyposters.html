TYPOGRAPHY POSTERS

Programs: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

This is a mockup render which I created to advertise a fictional typography exhibit, with each poster using only a single typeface and briefly summarizing it (in addition to giving event details). The left design uses Dunbar, a typeface I enjoyed for its long characters that could extend to the edges of the poster. I ended up being very playful with design, using many variations of the typeface (and even punching through other letters) to stylize different information and pieces of the title. The right design uses Filson Soft, which I enjoyed for its modern look that avoided serif clutter. This lack of clutter helped to support the poster’s main gimmick, which is a sort of futuristic fading or repeating effect that compliments the type well (as do the futuristic bright teals and blues.)
