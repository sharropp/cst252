TASTE TEA

Programs: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

These are mockup renders which I created for a fictional brand called Taste Tea, which seeks to “re-envision tea for a modern audience” in a way that’s “Bold. Flavorful. Fun!”. To help add excitement to my tasty flavors, I created a bunch of cute fruit mascots called the Taste Tea Team, who represent different teas. The mockups depict the packaging for the ParTea Pack (a three pack containing Lemon-Lime Lift, Raspberry Rave, and Cherry Chillin’) as well as some merchandise mugs with Team Flavor and the Taste Tea logo (plus themed tea tags). In addition to these mockups, I created a series of different hypothetical advertisement and promotion posters for various social media platforms, including Youtube, Instagram, and Twitter
